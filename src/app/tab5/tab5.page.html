Student Health

GMIT provides many different services to help you look after you’re physical and mental health.


Health Unit


The Student Health Unit provides professional and confidential medical care to GMIT students.


To make an appointment please call +353 (0) 91 74 2228.


Please bring a valid GMIT student ID Card with you when attending your appointment.


Location: Near the Student’s Union

Hours: 9am – 6pm Monday to Thursday 9am – 5pm on Friday

Phone: +353 (0) 91 74 2228

Email: studenthealthunit@gmit.ie


For medical emergencies, call: 087 9971574


Counselling


GMIT offers professional and confidential counselling for free to all students.


Whatever your concerns, GMIT has a trained professional who can help you work through your problems and help you develop more effective ways of coping.


To request an appointment with a counsellor you can stop by at the Student Services Office, phone, or email.


Room Number: 162

Phone: +353 (0)91 74 2118

Email: counselling@gmit.ie
